About Greenline

Greenline News is a beginner-friendly live market news platform that combines real-time stock data, AI-powered article explanations, and personalized investing guides. Designed for everyday investors who want to understand the market without the jargon.

"The market moves fast. Greenline is built to make sure nobody gets left behind."
