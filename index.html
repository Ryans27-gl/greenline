Welcome to Greenline
Your beginner-friendly stock market news hub
Greenline makes it easy to stay on top of market news — even if you're completely new to investing. No jargon, no complexity.
📰
Real news, real time
Fresh financial headlines from Reuters, Bloomberg, and the Wall Street Journal.
📈
Built for beginners
Every feature is designed to be simple, clear, and approachable — no trading experience needed.
💹
News meets stock data
See exactly which stocks each story might affect
Under every article you'll find a widget showing the stocks most likely to be affected — with live prices and % change.
🟢
Green means up, red means down
Stock chips show the % change in price. Green = stock went up. Red = it went down. Simple as that.
🔄
Always fresh
Hit Refresh any time to pull the latest headlines. The feed updates automatically every 5 minutes.
💡
Two powerful tools
Explain and Watchlist — your investing sidekicks
Greenline has two features that set it apart from every other news app out there.
🤖
AI Explain button
Confused by a headline? Hit Explain and AI breaks it down in plain English — what happened and what it means for investors.

Your Watchlist
Add any stock to your personal watchlist to track its price, % change, and mini chart all in one place.
